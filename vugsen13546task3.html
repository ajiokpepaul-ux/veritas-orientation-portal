Common School Terms Explained

Lecture
- A teaching session where an instructor presents course material to students
Assignment
- A task given to students to complete, usually graded for academic credit
Semester
- An academic term lasting approximately 15-18 weeks
Transcript
- An official record of a student's grades and academic performance
Syllabus
- A document outlining course objectives, schedule, and grading policies
Prerequisite
- A course that must be completed before enrolling in a more advanced course
