Student Academic Resources

Required Materials
  • Notebooks and folders
  • Pens and highlighters
  • Textbooks and reference books
  • Laptop or tablet
  • USB drive for backups
Steps to Register for Classes
  1. Log into the student portal
  2. Check registration eligibility
  3. Select courses from the catalog
  4. Meet with academic advisor
  5. Confirm course selection
  6. Pay tuition fees
  7. Print class schedule
Key Academic Terms
GPA (Grade Point Average)
- A numerical average of all course grades, used to measure academic performance
Credit Hour
- A unit that represents one hour of classroom instruction per week
Prerequisite
- A course that must be completed before enrolling in a more advanced course
Elective
- A course that students can choose freely, not required for their major
Transcript
- An official record of all courses taken and grades earned
Syllabus
- A document outlining course objectives, assignments, and grading policies
